# Germany Wedding Trip Packing List

Trip: Thursday, May 14 - Monday, May 18  
Vibe: cute, warm, rain-ready, garden-formal wedding, walkable city days.

## Outfit Plan

### Thu May 14 - Drive to Munich + Madison Beer Concert

- [ ] Cute comfy travel outfit: soft trousers or dark jeans, nice tee/tank, cardigan or light sweater
- [ ] Concert layer: leather jacket, bomber, trench, or wool-blend coat depending on weather
- [ ] Waterproof or water-resistant cute shoes you can stand in
- [ ] Small concert bag/crossbody
- [ ] Optional upgrade: sparkly top, ribbon, statement earrings, glossy lip
- [ ] Backup socks in day bag

Weather: cool and showery, around 12 / 6 C. Keep umbrella and rain jacket reachable, not buried in the trunk.

### Fri May 15 - Wedding at Schloss Jagersburg

- [ ] Main garden-formal outfit: flowy midi/maxi dress, elegant jumpsuit, or skirt/top set
- [ ] Warm layer: wrap, shawl, cropped cardigan, blazer, or coat that still looks intentional
- [ ] Closed-toe or secure shoes for damp grounds, cobblestones, gravel, and hard castle floors
- [ ] Small dressy bag or clutch
- [ ] Nude or matching tights/stockings if your outfit works with them
- [ ] Jewelry
- [ ] Hair accessories
- [ ] Wedding card/envelope or gift
- [ ] Cash/coins for small expenses
- [ ] Small umbrella that will not ruin photos
- [ ] Blister plasters
- [ ] Makeup touch-up kit
- [ ] Flat backup shoes in the car for dancing or late-night exit

Weather: cool and rainy, around 14 / 5 C. Ceremony is indoors, drinks/photos may be outside.

### Sat May 16 - Nuremberg Day

- [ ] Cute city outfit: jeans/trousers or a skirt with tights, sweater or fitted knit
- [ ] Rain jacket or trench
- [ ] Comfortable flat shoes with grip
- [ ] Crossbody/day bag
- [ ] Warm scarf
- [ ] Optional cute photo layer: structured jacket, beret, or cardigan

Weather: cloudy with light rain, around 13 / 7 C. Cobblestones can be slippery.

### Sun May 17 - Drive to Salzburg + Old Town Dinner

- [ ] Comfortable drive outfit: leggings/trousers/jeans, knit, warm socks
- [ ] Real coat for Salzburg evening
- [ ] Cute dinner top or sweater
- [ ] Rain-friendly shoes
- [ ] Umbrella in easy reach

Weather: rainy and cold, around 12 / 3 C. This is the coat day.

### Mon May 18 - Salzburg Altstadt + Fortress + Drive Home

- [ ] Layered walking outfit: tee or thin knit, sweater/cardigan, jacket/coat
- [ ] Comfortable walking shoes
- [ ] Sunglasses if it clears
- [ ] Day bag for camera, wallet, water, scarf
- [ ] Fresh top for the drive home if wanted

Weather: mostly dry and milder, around 18 / 1 C. Chilly morning, nicer afternoon.
