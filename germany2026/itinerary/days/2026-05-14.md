<p class="charge-warning"><strong>Extra charges to watch:</strong> the Slovenian hybrid already has the Slovenian vignette, so the main road charges are <strong>Austria motorway vignette</strong> plus separate tunnel tolls: expect <strong>Karawanken tunnel</strong> and likely <strong>A10 Tauern/Katschberg tunnel tolls</strong>. A normal car has <strong>no German motorway toll</strong>. You <strong>do not need the Munich green Umweltplakette for Zenith / MOC</strong>, but you <strong>do need it if you drive inside the Mittlerer Ring</strong> — including the previous Münchner Freiheit idea. Budget for paid parking in Munich/Salzburg. Check/buy Austrian tolls via <a href="https://shop.asfinag.at/en/">ASFINAG</a> before leaving.</p>

<p><strong>Weather note:</strong> Munich is cool and showery (~12°/6°) — pack a rain jacket and umbrella in your day bag, not deep in the trunk. The Zenith is fully indoor so the concert itself isn't a problem, but parking, walking to the venue, and the queue can be wet and cold. Warm layer + waterproof shoes.</p>
