<p>Checkout at 11 AM, then leave the car in a central garage and properly explore Salzburg before driving home in the late afternoon. The drive south is only ~3 h 15 min so there's plenty of day for the Altstadt, the Festung, and one last coffee.</p>

<p><strong>Weather note:</strong> the one good day of the trip — milder and mostly dry, ~18°/1°. This is the day to do the outdoor stuff: the Festung Hohensalzburg terrace and the views from the Mönchsberg are the payoff. Mornings will still be chilly (single digits), so warm layer to start, peel down through the afternoon.</p>
