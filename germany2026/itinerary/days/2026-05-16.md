<p>Day after the wedding. Sleep in, eat properly, then a half-day in Nuremberg — old town, the Imperial Castle, and a plate of Nürnberger Rostbratwürste. Heroldsbach is ~35 min from the Altstadt, so this is easy to do at half-speed. Do any grocery/pharmacy shopping today if you need it: German shops are generally closed on Sundays, and smaller towns get very quiet.</p>

<p><strong>Kat's nearby alternatives:</strong> if you do not feel like Nuremberg, the wedding site especially recommends <a href="https://www.google.com/maps?q=Bamberg%20Altstadt">Bamberg old town</a> for the storybook UNESCO feel and smoked beer, <a href="https://www.google.com/maps?q=W%C3%BCrzburg%20Residence">Würzburg Residence</a> as her favorite palace/UNESCO site in the region, or <a href="https://www.google.com/maps?q=Fr%C3%A4nkische%20Schweiz">Franconian Switzerland</a> for countryside, castles, and short hikes. The rural options are much easier by car.</p>

<p><strong>Weather note:</strong> cloudy with light rain, ~13°/7°. Rain jacket + small umbrella. Most of this plan tolerates rain well — the Bratwurst spots, Lebkuchen cafés, and the Kaiserburg interior rooms are indoor. If it really pours, drop the Altstadt loop and spend more time inside the castle palas and Albrecht-Dürer-Haus. Cobbles get slippery; flat-soled shoes.</p>
