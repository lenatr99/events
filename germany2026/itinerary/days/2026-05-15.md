<p>Wedding day at <a href="https://maps.app.goo.gl/weyfxWsy1FcU6oEv8">Schloss Jägersburg</a> in Eggolsheim — a small Baroque hunting castle with a chapel, gardens, and a brewery. About a 15-minute drive from the Heroldsbach cabin.</p>

<p><strong>Weather note:</strong> forecast is cool and rainy — about 14°/5°C. The <em>ceremony is indoors</em> (chapel), but the <em>drinks reception is outside</em> and Kat & Gil's photo session is on the grounds. Bring a small umbrella you can stash, a warm layer under the blazer / wrap for the dress, and closed shoes — heels and gravel are bad enough dry, worse damp. The castle has hard floors so wet shoes will be noticed.</p>

<p><strong>Wedding website notes:</strong> ceremony starts at <strong>14:00 sharp</strong> in the castle chapel, so arrive early. Dress code is <strong>garden formal</strong>: flowy midi/maxi dresses, elegant jumpsuits, or seasonal suits are perfect; men need a blazer with tailored trousers, tie optional. The ceremony is symbolic Catholic-style and mostly German, with an English service booklet. Keep some cash/coins for small bakeries, bathrooms, or tips; if bringing a card/envelope gift, that is welcome too.</p>
