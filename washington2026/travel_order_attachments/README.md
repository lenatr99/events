# Travel Order Attachments Checklist

Use this folder as the checklist for documents to attach to the FRI travel order for ISMB 2026.

## Attach Now

- ISMB 2026 conference homepage or event description
  - Attach: `01_ISMB_2026_Conference_Overview.pdf`
- ISMB 2026 abridged agenda
  - Attach: `02_ISMB_2026_Abridged_Agenda.pdf`
- Poster acceptance / presenter confirmation
  - Attach: `03_ISMB_2026_Poster_Acceptance_Email.pdf`
- ISMB 2026 registration fee information
  - Attach: `04_ISMB_2026_Registration_Fees.pdf`
- ISMB 2026 venue/accommodation/travel page
  - Attach: `05_ISMB_2026_Venue_Accommodation_Travel.pdf`
- ISMB 2026 conference fellowship information, if mentioning pending fellowship
  - Attach: `06_ISMB_2026_Conference_Fellowship_Info.pdf`
- ISMB 2026 fellowship invitation email
  - Attach: `07_ISMB_2026_Fellowship_Invitation_Email.pdf`

## Add When Available

- Exact poster title or poster submission confirmation.
- ISMB registration confirmation or invoice.
- Flight quote or booking confirmation for Ljubljana - Washington, DC - Ljubljana.
- Washington hotel quote or booking confirmation.
- Travel insurance quote or policy.
- Any proof of payment or invoice for reimbursable poster printing, ESTA, luggage, or payment fees, if claimed.

## Do Not Attach As Reimbursable

- Morgan Wallen concert ticket or concert-related costs.
- Baltimore hotel booking.
- Baltimore trains or local Baltimore transfers.

These are personal and should not be included in the employer reimbursement estimate.
