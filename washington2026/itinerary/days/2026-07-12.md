<details class="stay-details" open>
  <summary>Getting there — Ljubljana to the Washington Hilton</summary>
  <div class="stay-details-body">
    <div class="detail-grid">
      <div class="detail-item"><span>From</span><strong>Ljubljana (LJU)</strong></div>
      <div class="detail-item"><span>Arrive at</span><strong>Washington Dulles (IAD)</strong></div>
      <div class="detail-item"><span>Routing</span><strong>1 stop via Frankfurt or Munich</strong></div>
      <div class="detail-item"><span>Total travel</span><strong>~13-15 hours</strong></div>
      <div class="detail-item"><span>Time change</span><strong>DC is 6 h behind Ljubljana</strong></div>
      <div class="detail-item"><span>Airline deals</span><strong>United link / Delta code NM3UP</strong></div>
    </div>
    <p><strong>Flight.</strong> There are no nonstop flights from Ljubljana to the US, so the cleanest routing is <strong>LJU → Frankfurt (FRA) or Munich (MUC) → Washington Dulles (IAD)</strong> — one stop, roughly 13-15 hours gate to gate. Ticketing it as a Star Alliance itinerary (Lufthansa feeder plus United transatlantic) lets you use the ISMB <a href="https://www.united.com/en/us/book-flight/united-reservations?txtPromoCode=ZRZM473877">United discount link</a>. If you route via Paris (CDG) on SkyTeam instead, book through <a href="https://www.delta.com/flight-search/book-a-flight?tripType=ROUND_TRIP&amp;priceSchedule=price&amp;originCity=&amp;destinationCity=&amp;departureDate=&amp;returnDate=&amp;paxCount=1&amp;meetingEventCode=NM3UP&amp;searchByCabin=true&amp;cabinFareClass=BE&amp;deltaOnlySearch=false&amp;deltaOnly=deltaPartner">Delta with meeting code NM3UP</a>. IAD is the recommended arrival airport — DCA is closer but mostly domestic, and BWI adds an extra train transfer.</p>
    <p><strong>IAD to the venue by train.</strong> Dulles has its own Metro station on the <strong>Silver Line</strong>, linked to the main terminal by a covered walkway. Ride the Silver Line toward downtown to <strong>Metro Center</strong> (about 55 min), transfer to the <strong>Red Line</strong> toward Shady Grove, and go two stops to <strong>Dupont Circle</strong>. Leave by the north / Q Street exit and walk about 0.5 mi (10 min) north on Connecticut Ave NW to the Washington Hilton. Total roughly 1 h 15 to 1 h 30; about $6-8 with a SmarTrip card or a contactless tap.</p>
    <p><strong>IAD to the venue by car.</strong> Taxi or rideshare is about 26 miles and 35-50 min depending on traffic, roughly $45-75 — the simplest option after a long-haul flight.</p>
    <p><strong>Arrive a day early.</strong> Transatlantic flights from Europe land in DC in the afternoon or evening, and July 12 programming starts at 08:00. Flying in on Saturday, July 11, gives you a buffer for the 6-hour time change and for poster setup.</p>
  </div>
</details>
