LOCATION: Columbia Ballroom

We require that in-person presenters upload, at minimum, a pdf or an mp4, but prefer you upload both so that our virtual attendees get the most from your work. Virtual presenters are required to upload both.

Files will be uploaded to the virtual conference platform no later than July 03, 2026. The video is part of the video library and all registered delegates will be able to view their poster through the conference platform. Only in-person poster presenters are required to have a physical poster to display in Washington, D.C.

Posters cannot exceed 46" x 46" (117cm x 117cm)

Click to enlarge

For optimal viewing, the videos should be recorded in 720p - 1080p and there is no limit on file size. Details on preparing your poster recordings can be found at the Presenter Information page.

NOTE: Poster numbers may change without notice, check back to confirm number prior to putting up poster.
